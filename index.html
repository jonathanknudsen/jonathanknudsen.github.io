Jonathan Knudsen is a versatile, deeply knowledgeable technologist with exceptional communication skills. He has a wide spectrum of experience in computing and networking, from security testing through software development. He has worked on software for transportation logistics, set top boxes, and smartphone apps. Jonathan used fuzz testing to locate vulnerabilities in mobile phones, network storage appliances, medical devices, and LTE infrastructure.

Jonathan is a voracious learner. He can hit the ground running with new technologies and concepts. He enjoys teaching others and has a talent for making complex technical material clear and comprehensible. Jonathan is the author of several books and more than one hundred articles about software security, Java technology and mobile robots. He is also a talented speaker.

Jonathan has a degree in mechanical and aerospace engineering from Princeton University. He lives in North Carolina with the most glorious woman to walk the Earth.
